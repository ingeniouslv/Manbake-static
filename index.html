Manbake

Manbake is a company based out of Las Vegas, Nevada. Our main objective is to provide progressive nutrition with gourmet flavors. We take old processed recipes and boost them with nutrients, while also omitting unhealthy additives, sugars and preservatives.

We use a lot of progressive ingredients, super foods and clean oils that provide a plethora of nutritional value. We use chia seeds and flax seeds in almost all of our products. We only use grape seed oil, evening primose oil and coconut oil. We use hemp! Hemp contains every single one of your amino acids, even some your body can't create! We use a variety of nuts and seeds, peanuts, sunflower seeds, pepitas, almonds, cashews and pecans. We're nuts about nutrition!

Beefcakes are our staple product. Don't be alarmed, there is no beef in them. They are all vegetarian friendly. Beefcakes are high in protein, low in carbs and gluten free! Not only do we omit damaging sugars, but we also utilize several super foods in the creation of a Beefcake. They are delicious and extremely healthy, and they keep you full and satiated for hours. We pack them with clean energy and only use the freshest natural and organic ingredients. And the taste... nothing compares.

All Beefcakes are made with no preservatives. They DO have a short shelf life. We will work diligently to ensure that when you purchase your beefcakes, you're getting the freshest products possible. We have to ship them in dry ice to preserve their integrity.

If you encounter spoiled product, please notify us immediately at info@manbake.us. We will make sure you either get your money back or we can discuss giving you credit on a future order while also replacing the products. We are devoted to making your Beefcake experience immensely pleasurable.
